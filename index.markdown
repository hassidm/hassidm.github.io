Hi there, I'm Michael.

I am a Research Scientist at Meta Superintelligence Labs ([MSL](https://ai.meta.com/)), working under the supervision of [Prof. Yaron Lipman](https://www.wisdom.weizmann.ac.il/~ylipman/). My research focuses on making large language models (LLMs) more efficient and advancing their modeling capabilities, specifically targeting inference-time optimization and architectural development. I'm also passionate about LLMs for reasoning, code generation, and mathematics, as well as speech language models (SLMs). For further details, please see my [publications](publications).

Prior to that, completed my PhD in Computer Science at the Hebrew University of Jerusalem ([HUJI](https://www.cs.huji.ac.il)), as part of a joint excellence program between [HUJI](https://www.cs.huji.ac.il) and [Meta AI](https://ai.meta.com/). During this time, I was guided by [Prof. Roy Schwartz](https://schwartz-lab-huji.github.io/) and [Dr. Yossi Adi](https://www.cs.huji.ac.il/~adiyoss/).

Before this, I spent almost four years at Google ([Search Console](https://search.google.com/search-console/about) and [Google research](https://research.google/)) as a software engineer and a researcher (part-time and full-time internships). My academic background includes a B.Sc. (**summa cum laude**, "Amirim" excellence program) and an M.Sc. (**magna cum laude**, excellence program) in Computer Science from HUJI. More details are available in my [CV](assets/docs/cv.pdf) (updates are infrequent).

Other than that, I consider my marriage to [Miri Varshavsky (now also) Hassid](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=6apvstwAAAAJ&hl=en) to be my greatest personal achievement. I also occasionally participate in spoken word performances.
